      • 1. Maximalism Minimalism has long been treated as a foundational element in the temple of design. But sometimes less isn’t more, more is more. Welcome to maximalism, a book cover design language that revels in abundance. We’re talking big and bold, intricate and complex, a kaleidoscope of colours. Maximalism delivers the very best type of assault on the eyes – one that pulls you in then keeps you there. These book cover designs offer more options: they can more literally embody the novels they wrap, or they can deliver narratives all their own.
