New York's Messages

New York's Messages is a collection of messages found around the city. It brings together the statements the city makes to the general public. Messages that are kind, funny or even provocative. The collection is organized by page, with each message having its own photo and text, all on a single web page. Each text is written from the inspiration and interpretation of the message. Whether straight to the point, a reminder or even a lesson.
