I'm at the pool

40.73513° N, 73.99157° W

Life often invites us to dive deeper than the surface. Just as the playful response “No, I'm at the pool” reminds us to enjoy the moment, we can find joy in the unexpected. Love may not always be the answer we're looking for, but connection and laughter can transform the ordinary into the extraordinary. Let's cherish those delightful little encounters that remind us to live fully and joyfully, wherever we are.
